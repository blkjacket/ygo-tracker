    🎤 Voice Commands
    Turn & Phase
    "next turn"Advance to next player's turn
    "previous turn"Go back a turn
    "next phase"Advance the current phase
    "battle phase"Jump to a specific phase (draw / standby / main one / battle / main two / end)
    Life Points
    "damage 800"Deal 800 to default opponent
    "800"Bare number — damage to opponent
    "deal 1500 to player 2"Deal LP to a specific player
    "hit all 1000"Deal to all non-active players
    "heal 500"Heal active player
    "heal player 2 two thousand"Heal a specific player
    "half"Halve active player's LP
    "undo"Undo last LP change
    "reset lp"Restore all LP to starting amount
    "transfer lp"Open LP transfer panel
    Timer
    "start timer"Start / resume the turn timer
    "pause"Pause the turn timer
    "stop timer"Stop and reset the turn timer
    Coin & Dice
    "flip coin"Open coin/dice and flip
    "roll dice"Open coin/dice and roll
    Game Actions
    "surrender"Active player concedes
    "eliminate player 2"Manually eliminate a player
    "revive"Second chance for active player
    "three players"Set player count (2–6)
    "new game"Confirm and start a new game
    "rematch"Same players, fresh LP
    "add counter"Add a counter token to active player's card
    Modals & Navigation
    "calculator"Open the LP calculator
    "game log"Open the game log
    "match record"Open match history
    "card search"Open card search
    "deck builder"Open card search + deck builder
    "rulings"Open the rulings panel
    "roster"Open the player roster
    "sync"Open multiplayer sync panel
    "fullscreen"Toggle fullscreen
    "close"Close the active modal
    Rulings
    "ask the ruling"Submit current ruling query to AI
    "add chain link"Add a link to the chain
    "remove chain link"Remove last chain link
    "clear ruling"Reset the ruling builder
